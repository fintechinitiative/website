Our Story

From Viv

In the summer of 2024, I traveled to China to teach the first class of our financial literacy program to a group of underserved students and I saw firsthand how important learning finance is from a young age. During the next school year, I founded the FinTech Initiative with our goal to continue teaching financial literacy to make this impact in my own community. I asked Andie if she wanted to help me create a program in New Haven. She agreed and brought the idea of including digital literacy as the two are becoming more and more intertwined. Together, we built our organization to reach as many schools as we can.

Our Mission

To empower young minds with essential financial and digital literacy skills, equipping them with the knowledge to manage money wisely and the technical skills to thrive in a digital world.
Through engaging, hands-on programs, we teach young students the fundamentals of financial responsibility and coding, fostering confidence and future-ready skills.

Our Impact
